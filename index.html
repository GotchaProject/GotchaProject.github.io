GOTCHA DB

GOTCHA database is used for scientific research of biometrics and soft biometric recognition in-the-wild. It is collected by Biometric Image Processing Lab (BIPLab) of University of Salerno in collaboration with Instituto Universitario de Sistemas Inteligentes y Aplicaciones Numéricas en Ingeniería (SIANI) of University of Las Palmas De Gran Canaria. Any one or group is allowed to use this database free of charge.
